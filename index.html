About FreButique

This project was born out of my desire to support my sister, Fre, in her dream of running her own women's clothing store. She's always had a passion for fashion and empowering women through style, and I wanted to help her bring that vision to life. Throughout the past 2 weeks, I've been pouring my skills into developing this ecommerce platform as a way to showcase her amazing products to the world. This is a Portfolio Project for alx-swe, a learning experience to demonstrate my web development capabilities.

**FreButique is dedicated to providing high-quality, stylish clothing for women of all shapes and sizes. We believe that fashion is a way to express yourself and feel confident, and we want to help you find the perfect pieces to make you look and feel your best.
